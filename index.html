About This Site

source & Methodology

This Q&A collection is designed to reflect the central source of Islam: the Qur'an, the authentic Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the understanding of the early Muslim community and leading Sunni Imams.

Where issues admit more than one valid scholarly view, we aim to indicate this clearly and present answers that are respectful of the four Sunni schools of law (Ḥanafī, Mālikī, Shāfiʿī, Ḥanbalī), avoiding harshness and polemics.

Clarity with Reverence

English explanations are written to be clear and accessible while preserving the dignity of the sacred texts. Key verses and narrations are mentioned with references (for example, "Qur'an 2:183" or "Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī").

This site is intended as a learning aid, not as a replacement for qualified scholars. For complex personal cases, we encourage you to speak directly with reliable people of knowledge in your community.

Important Disclaimer

The answers presented here are based on recognized source and mainstream Sunni scholarship, but they are general educational guidance. They may not capture every nuance of your personal situation.

Always verify important religious decisions with trustworthy local scholars who understand your circumstances and follow established Islamic legal methodology.
